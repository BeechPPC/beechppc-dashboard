## üéØ What It Does

When you say **"Do a clarity report on [URL]"**, the skill will:

1. Fetch and analyze the target website
2. Extract insights across 6 key dimensions:
   - Why the business exists
   - Who they serve
   - What they do
   - Their unique selling points
   - Proof and credibility factors
   - PPC strategy opportunities

3. Generate two professional reports:
   - Google Slides presentation (.pptx)
   - Comprehensive PDF report

4. Apply Beech PPC branding (your colors and style)
